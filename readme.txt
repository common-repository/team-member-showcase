== Description ==

Team Member Showcase is a super modern Team Member wordpress plugin. With this plugin you can display a set of pictures and information in different layouts. The main purpose of the plugin is to display team/staff members. Unlimited Colors, 100% responsive, automatic resize images, flexible, unlimited items, shortcode powered, custom link and more.


See live demo here: <a href="http://demo.codecans.com/plugins?theme=team-member-showcase">http://demo.codecans.com/plugins?theme=team-member-showcase</a>

== Installation ==

<p>Installation of <strong>Team Member Showcase</strong> is very simple.</p>

<strong>Option 1</strong>

<li> Download the plugin team-member-showcase.zip from download location.</li>
<li> Unpack the *.zip file and extract the Team Member Showcase.</li>
<li> Drop the Team Member Showcase plugin folder into your wp-content/plugins folder.</li>
<li> In word press administration panels, click on Plugins from the menu.</li>
<li> You should see your plug-in listed under Inactive plug-in tab.</li>
<li> To turn the word presses plug-in on, click activate.</li>

<strong>Option 2</strong>

<li> Download the plugin Team Member Showcase Plugin from download location.</li>
<li> Go to Add New menu under Plugins tab in your word-press admin.</li>
<li> Select upload link (top link menu).</li>
<li> Upload the available RD Hover Effects Plugin file and click install now</li>
<li> Finally click activate plug-in link to activate the plug-in.</li>


<h3>Settings Page</h3>	
<p>
Under <strong>Settings -&gt; Team Member Showcase plugin</strong> you can configure the basic settings. Everything else can be done on the fly via Custom post named<strong> Team Member Showcase</strong> on your Dashboard and via the Shortcode option on any Page or Post that you want.
</p>

<p>
<strong> Add Team Member Showcase- </strong>Allows you to add team member item to Custom Posts as well as add information such as:  Member Name, Member Details, Member Image, in the Team Member Showcase custom post.
</p>

<strong>Using the Team Member Showcase Plugin</strong></p>

<p>
 Once you see custom post name <strong>Team Member Showcase </strong>On your Dashboard left under <strong>pages.</strong> just click Add New Item :
	
</p>

<p>
You will see the Team Member information option panel with <strong>4 Field</strong>
</p>

<p>
1. <strong>First field</strong>: "Upload Member Image" Click to choose a file, the image can come from any source and be any size, you can also use images uploaded right from the post media uploader. Just copy the URL of the image after you upload it. </p>

<p>
2. <strong>Second field</strong>: "Enter Member Name" You can enter any Name which appear on Member Name.
</p>

<p>
3. <strong>Third field</strong>: "Enter Member Label" You can enter any Laber Ex: "Ceo & Developer"  which appear on Member Label.
</p>

<p>
4. <strong>Fourth field</strong> "Enter Your Description" You can quickly insert the description in the description text area. It is important to enter a short description to maintain the layout of the image wrapper.
</p>

<p>
After all of the steps above are done you will see a Menu named <strong>Add more Team Member item</strong>. If you want to add more items just click <strong>Add more Team Member item</strong> and you will see another field open; you can then add unlimited items.

</p>



<p>When Team Member Showcase Created Sucessfully done, just Click in Publish Button, You will see a shortcode which is created avobe.  Now we have to add shortCode on post or page where we want to show.</p>



<p>Go to <strong>Dashboard</strong>, <strong>Click Ad New Post or page.</strong> </p>



<p>In your <strong>Post/Page Editor</strong> just paste the shortcode there.</p>


<p> EX:</p>

<pre>[team id='75']</pre>



<p>After All Above all just click <strong>publish </strong></p>


<h2>And See the Magic of All Team Member Showcase items, Enjoy!</h2>
